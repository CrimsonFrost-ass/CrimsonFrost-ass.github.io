Glitch Verse

Roleplay Project - Azure Studios

Glitch Verse is an immersive roleplay project I'm developing under Azure Studios, a creative hub I founded. It explores a universe shaped by science gone awry and digital anomalies, blending sci-fi, mystery, and fantasy elements. At its core is the story of Binary Steve, a failed experiment turned reluctant hero, navigating a fractured world to save others and confront the evils that created them.
